USD.AI Capture 03

This looks closer to an initial repricing wave than a mature RAVE-style squeeze

CHIP and RAVE share one important trait: the market infrastructure was built before the move. But the current USD.AI setup still looks like a first launch repricing event, not yet a delayed squeeze machine.

USD.AI vs RAVE

Question USD.AI (CHIP) RAVE
Were rails built before the move? Yes. Sale, pre-market, futures, DEX, then spot. Yes. Listing, routing, broader access, then delayed squeeze.
What phase is the token in now? Initial launch repricing. Post-setup squeeze and unwind cycle.
What is the biggest risk? Public-sale overhang and unstable first-day speculation. Concentration, leverage cascade, and exit-liquidity risk.
What would confirm another major leg? New exchange access plus contained sale-cohort selling. Renewed squeeze conditions with thin float and coordinated demand.
