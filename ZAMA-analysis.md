### 4. Technology Narrative: FHE (Fully Homomorphic Encryption)

**Zama is building the first FHE-based confidential blockchain protocol:**

- **Core Tech:** FHE enables smart contracts to compute on encrypted data without decryption
- **Architecture:** Off-chain coprocessors for heavy FHE + ZKPoK for public verifiability
- **Capabilities:** Confidential tokens, encrypted logic, programmable compliance
- **Current Performance:** ~20 TPS
- **2026 Target:** 1,000 TPS
- **Future Target:** 10,000+ TPS via dedicated FHE ASICs

**Competitive Landscape:**
- **Fhenix:** Also FHE for private smart contracts (FHE rollups/L2s)
- **Zama Advantage:** Live mainnet, fair auction launch, cross-chain coprocessor model

**Analysis:** FHE is cutting-edge cryptography. If successful, Zama could dominate the privacy sector. BUT:
- Technology is still early (20 TPS now, 1000 TPS target)
- Competition from established privacy protocols
- Regulatory uncertainty around privacy tokens
